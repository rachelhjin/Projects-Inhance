Inhance

Summary

Two-week partner project to create and develop an app that addresses the daily needs of users.

  • MY ROLE:
  • UX Designer
  • TOOLS:
  • Adobe XD
  • InVision
  • Google Drive

PROBLEM STATEMENT
When people are furnishing homes for the first time or when they are re-decorating rooms, it is often difficult to bring their visions to real life.
SOLUTION
Inhance is a platform that helps users bring to life a vision for their living space. By incorporating planning, shopping, and sharing into one app, users can experience a simpler way to interior design.

User Research

In order to figure out what our users need, we conducted research through interviews and surveys.
Using the main takeaways from the user research, we proceeded with creating the User Persona for our app.
To further empathisize with our user, we created a storyboard.

Prototype & Testing

Using all of the data we gathered, we created several iterations of the product before creating our final prototype.
